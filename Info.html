What is the game about?

The Four Corners of Glaschu is a web based, text adventure game set in an alternative universe in Glasgow, Scotland (Glaschu)


How to Play

The game is very simple and all you have you to do is enjoy yourself! But specifically read each block of text which will set the scenario with a list of various options which you can select by hitting the buttons. Depending on your actions in the game this will affect your outcome and your aim is to make it to the centre of Glachu! Good luck and have fun :D
